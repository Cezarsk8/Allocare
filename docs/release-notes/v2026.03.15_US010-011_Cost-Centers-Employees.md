# Release Notes — US010+011 / USFW004: Cost Centers & Employees

**Date:** March 15, 2026
**User Stories:** US010+011 (Backend), USFW004 (Frontend)

---

## What's New

Companies can now organize their workforce into **Cost Centers** (departments/areas) and register **Employees** with full lifecycle management. This is the foundation for future cost allocation — assigning provider service costs to specific people and departments.

Key capabilities:
- **Cost Centers**: Create, edit, activate/deactivate departments with unique codes per company
- **Employees**: Register employees with email, job title, hire date, and optional cost center assignment
- **Employee Lifecycle**: Terminate employees with a date, reactivate them, or simple activate/deactivate
- **Company Navigation**: New tab bar on company pages (Geral, Centros de Custo, Colaboradores)
- **Search & Filter**: Paginated tables with search by name/code/email and filter by status or cost center
- **Cost Center Dropdown**: When creating/editing an employee, a dropdown lists all active cost centers

---

## Technical Details

### Backend
- **Entities**: `CostCenter` (Code, Name, Description, IsActive) and `Employee` (Name, Email, CostCenterId, JobTitle, HireDate, TerminationDate, IsActive)
- **15 API endpoints** across 2 controllers: `CostCentersController` (7 endpoints) and `EmployeesController` (8 endpoints)
- **Migration**: `AddCostCentersAndEmployees` — CostCenters and Employees tables with unique indexes (CompanyId+Code, CompanyId+Email)
- **FK Behavior**: CostCenterId on Employee uses `SetNull` — deleting a cost center nullifies the reference on linked employees
- **Pagination**: Uses existing `PagedResult<T>` generic with server-side search/filter
- **NoteEntityType**: Extended with `Employee = 2` and `CostCenter = 3` for future notes support

### Frontend
- **19 new files**: Types (3), Services (2), Hooks (2), Schemas (2), Components (7), Pages (3)
- **Company Layout**: New `layout.tsx` at `/companies/[id]/` with header + `CompanyNav` tab bar — existing detail page refactored to be the "Geral" tab
- **Pagination Molecule**: Reusable `Pagination` component (Previous/Next with page info), first molecule in the design system
- **Employee Form**: Cost center dropdown populated from API (active cost centers only, up to 100)
- **Terminate Dialog**: Inline amber-styled form with date picker and confirmation

---

## Notes
- Cost centers and employees are company-scoped (multi-tenant isolated)
- Employee cost center assignment is optional — employees can exist without a cost center
- This release sets up the foundation for US012 (Employee Service Assignment & Cost Allocation)
- The Pagination molecule is now available for reuse in future paginated list pages
